Cloud viewer unhappy paths use one visual family: centered copy, a quiet icon
badge, one sentence of detail, and at most one action. Severity changes the icon
and subtle badge or border tone, not the whole surface. These fixtures keep the
states calm across empty, blocked, stale, loading, and signed-out paths. Titles are short labels without trailing periods; details are single sentences.

## Empty state family

Reusable centered states for empty notebook lists, comments, packages, outlines,
read-only notebooks, and workstation setup.
